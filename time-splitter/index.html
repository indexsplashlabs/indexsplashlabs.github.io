Time Splitter — Privacy Policy

Developer: IndexSplash Labs · Effective: 14 June 2026

Time Splitter does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

What the app stores

Your activity names, timers, sessions, notes, goals, and settings are stored locally on your device only. The app has no user accounts and does not operate any servers.

Data sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

Exports and backups

Any CSV, text, or JSON export or backup is a file you create and choose a location for through your device's system file picker. These files stay under your control; we never receive them.

Notifications

Notifications are local to your device and are used only to show and control an active timer.

Children

Time Splitter is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
