Money Manager — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Money Manager is a private, offline-first personal finance app for Android. Your ledger, savings accounts, budgets, goals, reminders and audit logs are stored on your device in a private database. The app is completely free — it contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no third-party analytics.

Data we collect

None. The developer does not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your personal or financial information on any server. There is no account to create and no sign-up required to use the app.

Where your data lives

  • On your device: everything you enter (transactions, accounts, budgets, goals, reminders, audit logs) is saved locally in the app's private database and never leaves your device unless you choose to export or back it up.
  • Optional Google Drive backup: if you connect Google Sign-In, the app uses it only to read and write an encrypted backup file in your own Google Drive App Data folder. The backup is encrypted with a passcode you choose before it is uploaded, and it is stored in your Drive account — not with the developer. The app does not use Firebase or Firestore.
  • Optional local/file exports: JSON, CSV, Excel, and PDF exports are written only to the location you select on your device or to your Drive.

What we do not do

  • We do not run our own cloud database for your finances.
  • We do not show ads or use any advertising identifier.
  • We do not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your transaction history with anyone.
  • We do not require an account to use the app.

Permissions

  • Notifications: to show bill / EMI reminders you set up.
  • Internet: used only for the optional encrypted Google Drive backup and restore. The app works fully offline without it.

Security

  • Optional app lock using your device biometrics and PIN / pattern / password.
  • Optional "hide app preview" screenshot protection in the recents screen.
  • Private savings can be hidden from the dashboard, reports, and exports until you authenticate to reveal them.
  • Google Drive backups are encrypted with a passcode you choose before upload.

Your choices and control

  • Export or permanently delete all of your data at any time from Data Controls inside the app.
  • Disconnect Google Sign-In from the More tab at any time.
  • Restore from a local JSON backup without using Google at all.

Children's privacy

Money Manager is a general-purpose finance tool and is not directed at children. It collects no personal data from anyone, including children.
