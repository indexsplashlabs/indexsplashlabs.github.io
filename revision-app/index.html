Privacy Policy — Revision App

Application ID: com.indexsplashlabs · Last updated: 15 June 2026

Revision App (“the app”, “we”) is a free, offline study app for spaced-repetition revision. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

Revision App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device. There is no account, no sign-in, no ads, no analytics, and no internet connection is used by the app.

What the app stores (on your device only)

The app stores the following locally, using the device’s app storage (Android Room database + Jetpack DataStore preferences):

  • Subjects, topics/revision items, and notes you create.
  • Review history and confidence ratings.
  • Settings: dark-mode preference, daily-reminder time, onboarding-completed flag.

This information never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

Backups and export (user-initiated)

You can export your data to a JSON backup file or a CSV file using the Android system file picker. You choose where the file is saved (e.g. device storage or a cloud drive you select). The app does not upload these files anywhere itself; what happens to a file after you save it is governed by the destination you pick.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

Permissions

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — used only to show the optional local daily-revision reminder you can enable in Settings. No data is collected through this permission.

The app declares no internet permission and performs no network communication. Other install-time permissions present in the build (for background scheduling of the optional reminder) do not access the internet and collect no data.

Data retention and deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you are in full control:

  • Delete individual subjects/topics inside the app (deleted items go to Archive and can be permanently removed there).
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device.

Children’s privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app’s data practices change in a future version, this policy will be updated and the “Last updated” date above will change.
